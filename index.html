    Welcome

    A Virtual Summer Camp

    What is this?

    4 weeks of online learning, collaboration, and Q&As with earth and space science professionals. At the end of the month put your new skills to use in a hackathon where you'll work on creative answers to the question, "If you could observe any location on the Earth at any time, what problems would you want to shed light on?"

    Who can sign up?

    This year, Planet Summer Camp is open to US-based students ages 13-16. In order to participate in the live Q&As, you'll need access to a computer and stable internet connection.
